Sahrawi Arab Democratic Republic (SADR / Western Sahara)


Disputed Country in Africa.


Capital City: Laayoune

Largest City: Laayoune

Population: 582,000 (as of 2019)

Currency: Sahrawi Peseta (EHP)

National Language: Modern Standard Arabic, Saharan Spanish

Top level Domain: .eh

Territory Disputes


20% of the territory (Southern Side) is controlled by the SADR, with the other 80% (North Side) being controlled by Morocco.
